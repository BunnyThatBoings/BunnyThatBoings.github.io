Efficient Demon List (v2.2)

The EDL is made by CircularCyclone, ThePastaNebula, & urcool626

EDL list mods: CircularCyclone & oish

The EDL was made because we didn't like the arbitrary restrictions that Pointercrate and the Global Demon List put on levels, as their rate status (which is decided by RobTop, who has basically no connection to modern top play). Additionally, we didn't agree with the methodology of the other lists, as they take too long to place levels, don't listen to actual top player feedback, and cater too much to players who are opinion lying.

Starting at 1%, for every percentage you get on a randomly chosen level, you must get increasingly higher percentages on more random levels until you either give up or get 100%.
